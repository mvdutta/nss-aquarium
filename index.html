Pro-Tips

For a healthy salt-water aquarium:

  • Keep the tank clean and change the water at least every two weeks.
  • Check the temperature EVERY DAY with a submerged or digital thermometer.
  • Water evaporates...so check the salinity levels regularly with your trusty refractometer!
